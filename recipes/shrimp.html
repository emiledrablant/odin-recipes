Some Spicy Shrimp

Some Spicy Shrimp is the shortest road to Heaven, at least for me. Why is that so, you ask? Simple enough: as I'm allergic to it, just eating one of these curly thingies would kill me in less than twenty minutes. Also, she didn't try to "get rid of me", I just wrote that to be more dramatic. We actually broke up when she refused to share some candies with me. I'm still a sugar digger to this very day.
